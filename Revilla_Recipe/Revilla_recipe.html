Mac and Cheese

Quick, Easy, and Cheesy

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Cheese

Directions

  1. Boil water in medium saucepan. Stir in macaroni. Cook 7 to 8 min. or until tender, stirring occasionally.
  2. Drain. Do not rinse. Return to pan.
  3. Add butter, milk and cheese; mix well.
